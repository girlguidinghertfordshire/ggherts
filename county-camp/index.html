One world, many celebrations - join the party!

Calling all unit leaders, it’s time to round up your girls and book your places for Girlguiding Hertfordshire’s county camp for 2026!

We’re packing the best of guiding into 1 epic week: think outdoor activities, campfires, challenges, crafts, laughter, and memories that’ll last a lifetime.

Spaces are limited and excitement is building â€” don’t miss out!

Let’s give our girls a camp they’ll never forget!

Rainbows

During this exciting, fun filled residential week, we will also be running 2 activity days for Rainbows, on Monday 27 July and Thursday 30 July from 9am-4pm.

Rainbows will enjoy outdoor activities, craft and a campfire. They will be required to bring their own packed lunch.

Leaders must accompany their Rainbows to maintain the minimum ratio in line with the Girlguiding adult/child ratios.

Brownies

During this camp, Brownies have the option to attend for either a 2 night (Wednesday, 2pm arrival - Friday) or 3 night (Sunday - Wednesday 11am departure) residential, staying either indoor or outdoor.

Guides, Rangers and young leaders

Guides, Rangers and young leaders will attend for a 6 day/5 night camp.

Guides without leaders can attend as part of a subcamp, however spaces are limited.

Rangers and young leaders with Lead Away permits can attend without leaders as part of the subcamp, and can also chose the catering option if they would prefer their meals be prepared for them.

Trefoil Guild

Trefoil Guild members are also welcome to join us. The accommodation page details the options available. Alternatively, you can join us as a day visitor.

Please contact countycamp@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk
