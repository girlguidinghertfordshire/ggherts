Youth opportunities team

The Youth Opportunities team support and assist leaders, 13+ Guides, Rangers and Young Leaders.

International team

Our role to bring international guiding to Hertfordshire and will help to support you plan an amazing adventure.

Outdoor activities

The outdoor activities team plan regular Competitions, information about Paddlesports, Walks and Apps which will help you when you are out and about.

Trefoil Guild

Trefoil members are adults who enjoy life, make friends, support others, and value making time to do something ‘for them’ — and if it’s a challenge, so much the better!
