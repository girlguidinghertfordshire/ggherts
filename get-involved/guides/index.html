Guides

Ages 10-14

In Guides you'll get out there and do something really different! Go on adventures and try new things. As you progress through Guides you'll make friends, learn, and share new skills and may fly down the occasional zip wire.

You'll explore new places, go on camps, holidays and trips. Work towards badges and you can even start volunteering by becoming a Rainbow or Brownie helper.
