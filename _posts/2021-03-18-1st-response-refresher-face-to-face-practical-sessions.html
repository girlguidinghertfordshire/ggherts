For those who completed their virtual 1st Response Refresher in 2020 but the skills check is outstanding, indoor face to face sessions can now be booked.  They can be inside or still outside or 1:1 if you wish.

There will be a small charge to be paid on the night to the trainer.

Contact details for arranging a skills check are set out below.
