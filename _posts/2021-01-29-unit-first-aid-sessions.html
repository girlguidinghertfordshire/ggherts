As so many leaders are embracing the virtual world the Challenge for this badge can be done remotely.

Invite the county's First Aid Adviser, Verity Maclachlan to your unit Zoom meeting to run a 40 minute First Aid session covering skills builders or adapted to suit your girls.

Girls could earn the I'm a Lifesaver badge.
