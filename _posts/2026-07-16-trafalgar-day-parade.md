Youth United Hertfordshire, led by the Lord Lieutenant of Hertfordshire, Robert Voss CBE CstJ, has invited Girlguiding Hertfordshire to take part in the Trafalgar Day Parade on Sunday 25 October at Hatfield House.

This military and civic commemoration of the Navy's 1805 victory at the Battle of Trafalgar is a chance for young members to represent Girlguiding and take part in a longstanding naval tradition.

Each youth organisation has been allocated 24 spaces. Brownies, Guides, Rangers, young leaders and adult members are invited to apply by **Friday 11 September**.

Places will be allocated using a random selector, as with previous Youth United Hertfordshire events.

Further information regarding times will be circulated nearer the time.

If you are interested please apply below.
