Following last summer's successful golf sessions, this year, in partnership with The Golf Federation and Hertfordshire Golf, we are delighted to be able to offer further golf taster evenings, for Brownies and Guides.

All sessions are 1 hour in duration.

## Sessions

- **Tues 2 June:** Aldenham Golf Club, Radlett (Brownies 6.15pm - 7.15pm and Guides 7.30pm - 8.30pm).
- **Wed 3 June:** Stevenage Golf Club, Stevenage (Brownies 6.15pm - 7.15pm and Guides 7.30pm - 8.30pm).
- **Wed 10 June:** Chesfield Downs Golf Club, Hitchin (Brownies 6.15pm - 7.15pm and Guides 7.30pm - 8.30pm).
- **Wed 10 June:** Hadley Wood Golf Club, Barnet (Brownies 6.15pm - 7.15pm and Guides 7.30pm - 8.30pm).
- **Wed 17 June:** Berkhamsted Golf Club, Berkhamsted (Brownies and Guides 6.15pm - 7.15pm)
- **Wed 24 June:** Letchworth Golf Club, Letchworth Garden City (Brownies and Guides 6.15pm - 7.15pm)
- **Thurs 25 June:** The Hertfordshire Golf & Country Club, Broxbourne (Brownies and Guides 6.15pm - 7.15pm)
- **Thurs 25 June:** South Herts Golf Club, Totteridge, London (Brownies 6.15pm - 7.15pm and Guides 7.30pm - 8.30pm).

## Cost

£3 per girl. Leaders go free.

An information flyer with QR code linked to booking form is attached for you to download and share.

Please contact <daisy@golf-foundation.org> with any queries. Please ensure you read the terms and conditions set out in the booking form.
