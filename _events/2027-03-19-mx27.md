The Mountain Experience weekend MX27 is taking place from Friday 19 - Sunday 21 March 2027.

This fun, but challenging, residential weekend is aimed at Rangers and young leaders wanting to learn how to navigate using a map and compass, whether this be for their Duke of Edinburgh's Award (DofE)*, Peak Assault or personal adventures in the mountains.

*Excellent walking/navigation training for all DofE levels, especially Gold with indoor. Can be used in place of DofE Bronze training expedition.

More details to follow.
