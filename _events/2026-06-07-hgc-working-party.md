Come along on Sunday 7 June to help get our county centre, in Cottered, ready for our summer visitors.

Have fun with others as you help out at this social working party.

This is the perfect opportunity to see for yourself the changes and improvements that have taken place at The Hertfordshire Guide Centre during the recent modernisation and refurbishment.

The day will run from 10am - 4pm, but you can join us at any time. Simply let us know before Tuesday 2 June when we can expect to see you by completing this form:

[I would like to volunteer](https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=3yob_CzTykeMNWNnWM6OwZj-g9JL5lpMiAybQMCV5zxUOVVLNU83VEZSV0U0UDlENElSVENCSElTVy4u){:.btn.btn-primary.external-link}{:rel="noopener"}{:target="_blank"}

Refreshments will be provided, but please bring your own lunch if you'll be with us for the lunchtime period.

We hope to see you and thank you in advance for giving up your time and energy so others can enjoy HGC.

Enquiries to <HGCchair@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk>
