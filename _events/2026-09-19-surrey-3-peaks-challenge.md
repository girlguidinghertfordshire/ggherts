Are you ready to lace up your boots and take on a challenge?

The Herts walking team invites our county Rangers, young leaders and adults to join them for a spectacular day of hiking across the North Downs.

Experience the best of Surrey's landscapes as you tackle 3 iconic peaks in 1 day.

The perfect opportunity to test your endurance, meet fellow members from across the county, and enjoy the great outdoors!

### The challenge at a glance

Date: Saturday 19 September

Distance: 29km

Elevation: 800m of total ascent

Duration: approximately 8 hours

Cost: £5 entry fee

Who: Rangers, young leaders and adults (Rangers and young leaders will walk as a group with Grace from the walking team)

### Travel and logistics

We've kept it simple: park your car at the start point, enjoy the trek, and we will take a train back to the cars once we finish our 29km loop.

### Important details

Badges: We are looking into a badge with availability to be confirmed once we have final attendee numbers.
