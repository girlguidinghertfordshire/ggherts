**Save the date: Sensory Safari!**

A wild adventure for Rainbows and Brownies.
Get ready to use your super senses! Join us for a magical day of exploration where we'll discover nature in a whole new way. From the tiniest rustle in the grass to the brightest colours of autumn, there is so much to see, touch, smell, and hear.
Our Sensory Safari is designed specifically for our youngest members to get curious and get outdoors.

## Adventure details

- **When:** Sunday 17 October 2027
- **Who:** Rainbows and Brownies
- **Where:** location to be revealed soon.

## What's in store?

We'll be heading out on a "safari" like no other. Instead of binoculars, we'll be using our senses to track down the secrets of the forest:

- **👂 Listen:** can you hear the "crunch" of the autumn leaves or the call of a hidden bird?
- **🔍 See:** hunt for the most vibrant colours and teeny-tiny bugs.
- **🖐️ Touch:** feel the difference between rough bark and soft moss.
- **👃 Smell:** discover the earthy scents of the woodland in October.

Please email <walking@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk> with any queries.
