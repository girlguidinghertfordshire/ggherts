Are you ready to put your navigation skills to the test? Join the walking team for an exciting 8k cross-country trail through the beautiful Mardley Heath area on **Saturday 22 May 2027**!

This is the perfect chance to use that training you've been practicing and enjoy a brilliant day out in the fresh air.

**Key event info**

- **Start/finish:** Welwyn
- **The route:** An 8k trail, primarily on public footpaths.
- **Timing:** Staggered starts. Expect the hike to take approximately **3.5 - 4 hours**.

**Team and leader info**

- **Teams:** 4 (min) to 8 (max) girls per team.
- **Guides:** Must have at least one qualified leader per unit (plus parent helpers to meet the 1:12 ratio).
- **Rangers:** Very welcome to attend and do **not** need to bring a leader with them.
- **Leader roles:**

**Cost and admin**

- **Price:** £5 per girl (leaders are free!).
- **Forms:** Information and consent forms are required. Units must arrange their own home emergency contact.
- **How to book:** Booking form will be available nearer the event. Email **<walking@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk>** with the subject **'Incident hike'** in the meantime.

**Don't miss out!**

This is a fantastic opportunity for Guides and Rangers to build independence, teamwork, and outdoor confidence. Whether you're walking the trail or manning a checkpoint, we can't wait to see you there.

**Get your teams together and let's hit the trail!**
