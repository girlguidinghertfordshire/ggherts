The county Inspire team invites all Girlguiding Anglia Inspire members (aged 18-30) to their first Inspire weekend away.

Taking place in the beautiful City of Ely, in the scenic Cambridgeshire Fens and staying overnight in Ely Scout Hut from Saturday 16 to Sunday 17 May you will have the chance to explore the majestic Cathedral, Oliver Cromwell's House or visit the pretty riverside. Maybe you would prefer to visit the Sunday market. Other activities, crafts and games will be available for you to choose from and join in with and all attendees will receive a special badge to mark this event.

If you would like to find out more, or come along, please complete the [Ely Explorers register of interest form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeLTBR8nuZl0KKCmC6kNNOyKnoo09UBuexoReaDIWAtF78UZw/viewform){:.external-link}{:target="_blank"}{:rel="noopener"} before 1 May.

Enquiries to <inspire@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk>

A WhatsApp group will be set up for those registering an interest.
