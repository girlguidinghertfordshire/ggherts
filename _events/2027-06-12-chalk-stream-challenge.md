Splash into the Chalk Stream challenge!

This is a brilliant opportunity for Rainbows, Brownies and Guides to explore nature, learn about local heritage, and earn a [fantastic badge](https://www.girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk/unit/badges/#chalk-stream-challenge-badge){:.external-link}{:target="_blank"}{:rel="noopener"}.

Did you know that chalk streams are the UK's equivalent of a tropical rainforest? Join us at for a day of discovery as we dive into the rare and wonderful world of the River Ver.

## Event details

- **When:** Saturday 12 June 2027
- **Where:** St Albans
- **Who:** open to all Rainbows, Brownies, and Guides

## What's the challenge?

Become a chalk stream expert by taking part in fun, hands-on activities tailored for every age group:

- River geography
- Wildlife spotting
- Get creative
- River dipping

Ready to explore? Mark your calendars for **Saturday 12 June** and get ready for a day of river-side fun!

> "Discover the wonders of our rare and precious river heritage."

Contact <walking@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk>.
