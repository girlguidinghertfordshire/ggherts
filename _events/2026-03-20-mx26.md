Mountain Experience event (MX26) is taking place from Friday 20 - Sunday 22 March in the Peak District, staying at Whiteley Wood Outdoor Activity Centre.

This residential weekend is aimed at Rangers and young leaders wanting to learn how to navigate using a map and compass, whether this be for their Duke of Edinburgh’s Award (DofE)*, Peak Assault or personal adventures in the mountains.
*Excellent walking/navigation training for all DofE levels, especially Gold with indoor. Can be used in place of DofE Bronze training expedition.

Cost: £84 per person (includes transport, accommodation, food and training).

**Bookings for this year's event are now closed.**

Enquiries to <walking@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk>.
