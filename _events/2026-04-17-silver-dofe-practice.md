This Silver DofE practice expedition in the Chilterns, commencing on the evening of Friday 17 April and concluding on Sunday 19 April at approximately 4pm, consists of a short walk, overnight camp, a day walk on the Saturday, further overnight camp and a final day walk on the Sunday.  

Cost: £40 (including site fees, gas, equipment and petrol for supervisors).  

For more information or to book a place please email <dofe@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk>

Once you have successfully completed your Silver DofE practice expedition you can progress to the [Silver DofE qualifying expedition](/event/silver-dofe-qualifying/).

Please note this expedition is run in conjunction with the Bronze DofE qualifying expedition.

More information about DofE within Girlguiding and qualifying age criteria can also be found on our [DofE page](/youth-opportunities/dofe/).

**If you are unable to meet these dates, please advise the [county DofE Award lead volunteer](mailto:dofe@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk) as soon as possible so that other counties can be contacted to see what opportunities they have available.**
