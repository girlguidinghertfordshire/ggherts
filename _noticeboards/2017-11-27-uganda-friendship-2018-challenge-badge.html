In August 2018, three Leaders and twelve young women from Girlguiding Hertfordshire will travel to Uganda where they will complete a two week service project in an orphanage and local village community.  This includes teaching primary students, painting the school and building a toilet block.  It won’t all be hard work, as there will be fantastic opportunities for the group to attend cultural events and experiences, culminating in a three day safari.
