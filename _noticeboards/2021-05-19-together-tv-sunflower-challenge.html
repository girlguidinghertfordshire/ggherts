Together TV have launched the Sunflower Challenge, an initiative to distribute 400,000 free sunflower seeds across the UK to help people improve their mental health and connect with their community, running a 12 week growing journey fronted by TV gardener Danny Clarke.

You can register on an individual basis for free seeds, a weekly calendar, exclusive tutorials and friendly tips on how to grow them.  Registrants under 18 should buddy up with an adult (e.g. family member) who will sign up on their behalf.

More details and the link to register are available in the Sunflower Challenge Toolkit.
