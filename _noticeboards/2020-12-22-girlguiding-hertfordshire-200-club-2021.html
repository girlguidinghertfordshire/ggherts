Please support the Girlguiding Hertfordshire County Centre, Cottered.

Open to Girlguiding Hertfordshire members/units/districts/divisions it costs £15 a year giving the opportunity to join the monthly draws. There are two prizes of £20 and £15 every month with an extra prize of £200 in December and £75 in June. All proceeds go towards the upkeep of the County Centre.

For further details, terms and conditions and an application form,  please visit the Herts Guide Centre website or email 200@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk

If your application form is received before 30 January 2021 you will have the chance to be entered into the first draw of 2021.
