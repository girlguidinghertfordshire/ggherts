The May edition of the Rotary Times broadsheet details all of the programmes the Rotary Club of Stevenage hopes to run from September this year subject to Government advice.

A form to register interest in any programme detailed can be found on the last page of the Rotary Times broadsheet or an email may be sent direct to contact@stevenagerotary.org.uk.
