**Save the date.**

Ready to take your leadership skills to new heights?
Join the Herts walking team for an empowering weekend exploring the South Downs Way.
This is a dedicated opportunity to gain experience, clock up miles, and enjoy some of the UK's most stunning coastal and rolling hill scenery.

## The details

- **When:** Friday 4 - Sunday 6 June 2027
- **Where:** The South Downs Way (exact base location tbc)
- **Who:** Leaders and adults

**Why join us?**

- **Log those miles:** A perfect way to add countryside hours to your logbook.
- **Hands-on training:** Practice navigation and group management.
- **Fresh air and friendship:** Connect with other Herts leaders/adult volunteers while tackling one of England's most iconic National Trails.

This weekend can also be attended as a purely [social walking experience](/event/social-walking-weekend/).

Full details, kit lists, and booking information will be released soon.
