Calling all adventurers! Master the map, dust off your compass, and join this afternoon of navigation training for Guides, Rangers, and leaders.

Whether you're working towards your next badge, leading a unit expedition, or just want to stop relying on your phone's GPS, this afternoon is for you.

This would be good training for those undertaking their Bronze Duke of Edinburgh's Award and a fantastic opportunity for those planning to take part in the Incident hike in May 2027 to learn new skills or brush up on existing ones and get some practice under your belt. Working together with your team now means you'll be ready to smash those checkpoints when the big hike arrives.

Join the Herts walking team for a practical, hands-on session designed to get you navigating with confidence.

## The details

**When:** Sunday 14 March 2027  
**Time:** afternoon (specific timings to be confirmed)  
**Who:** open to all Guides, Rangers, young leaders, and leaders across Hertfordshire.  
**Where:** location reveal coming soon
