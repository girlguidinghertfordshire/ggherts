Would you like support with managing unit finances?

Worried about 2027 HQ subscriptions?

This session, taking place from 7.30pm - 9pm on Monday 20 April at Galley Hill School, Hemel Hempstead, Herts, HP1 3JY, will provide units with support in all matters financial, including budgets, accounts, gift aid, census and subs.

There will also be an open discussion for attendees to share thoughts and ask questions.

Attendees may wish to bring a notebook and pen with them, as well as their unit accounts, which can be looked at if time permits.

Refreshments will be provided.

On site parking available.

To book your place please email <janetacc@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk>.
