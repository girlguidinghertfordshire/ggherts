Delivery

Ordering Eco awards only: If you are ordering more than a few Eco awards and no county badges, these will be sent directly to you from Girlguiding trading services who have fixed prices for postage, rather than using your choice of delivery. Where this situation occurs, it will be made clear in the email you receive from the county badge secretary.

Ordering Eco awards and county badges together: If you are ordering county badges and Eco awards at the same time you may receive 2 packages, 1 from the county badge secretary and one from Girlguiding trading services. This will only happen if postage works out cheaper sending 2 packages rather than 1. Where this situation occurs, it will be made clear in the email you receive from the county badge secretary.

Please select how you would like to receive the items:

The badge secretary will contact you to arrange a suitable date and time for collection.

You will be contacted by the badge secretary with payment details and the final order total. Payment must be made before the items are dispatched.

Packages sent by the county badge secretary are posted weekly on Wednesdays.

Packages sent by Girlguiding trading services will be posted by trading when possible. While orders are normally sent out within a few days, there are sometimes delays and a time estimate is not always available. Delays occur most often around the start or end of terms and during holiday periods.
