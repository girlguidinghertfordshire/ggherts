### Insight and innovation pool sign up - have your say

If you would like to have your say and help Girlguiding shape the way forward, opt in to the Girlguiding insight and innovation pool from the marketing preferences area on GO.

By joining the pool, you can take part in important research, be the first to try out new things, and help Girlguiding better meet its members' needs.

More information can be found here: [Join our insight and innovation pool](https://www.girlguiding.org.uk/what-we-do/events-and-opportunities/event-and-opportunity-finder/join-our-insight-and-innovation-pool/){:.external-link}{:target="_blank"}{:rel="noopener"}
