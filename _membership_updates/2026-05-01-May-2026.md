### GO update – Supervision tab

Two new actions have been added to the needs supervision tab on GO. Commissioners can now view and edit details of individuals, plus add notes for volunteers and enquirers with active pending roles.

This will help manage active pending volunteers and enquirers, by keeping track of actions taken and enable information to be shared, where multiple volunteers are supporting an individual.

### 1st response training updated

Girlguiding's first aid qualification, known as 1st response training, has been updated. The syllabus has been updated in line with the Resuscitation Council UK's guidelines for all first aid providers in the UK. From 1 April if you book on to a Girlguiding 1st response course you'll cover the updated syllabus.

If you're booking a first aid course through an external provider, you should find a course that meets the requirements of the updated syllabus. However, courses booked before 1 April and completed before 1 July that met the previous syllabus will be accepted.
