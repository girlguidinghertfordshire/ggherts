Join Amplify, Girlguiding’s youth steering group!

National opportunity

We are excited to announce that Amplify, Girlguiding’s youth steering group, are looking for at least 10 more young people to join our returning Amplify members for 2023/2024.

Amplify was set up in July 2022 and are a group who come together virtually once a month to inspire and influence the decisions and projects that are important to girls directly within Girlguiding alongside staff and volunteers.

We’re looking for young people from all our counties and regions who represent the diversity of Girlguiding.

Applications now open. Closing 14 May 2023
