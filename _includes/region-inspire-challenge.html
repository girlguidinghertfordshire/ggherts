Region Inspire challenge project

The opportunity to be part of a county and region level project to create an adventurous challenge, highlighting the best parts of Hertfordshire and Anglia region, is open to our Inspire aged members (18-30), Queen's Guide award participants etc.

If you would like to be part of a working team, meeting about once a month, to create and test challenges around the county and beyond, aimed at Rangers, young leaders, Inspire, adult volunteers and Trefoil Guild members, please contact inspirechallenge@girlguiding-anglia.org.uk.
