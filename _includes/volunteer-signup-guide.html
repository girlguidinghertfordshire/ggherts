Register to volunteer with Girlguiding as a parent

All parents have access to GO, our membership system, so that they can see their daughter's details.

You should have received an invitation code via e-mail, if this has been lost it is possible to get sent a replacement.

Girlguiding home page

Go to go.girlguiding.org.uk and click on 'Sign in'

No invitation code?

Click on 'Don't have a login'

Enter your email address

NB must be the once you used to register your daughter – and ‘Submit’.

Click on 'Redeem invitation'

No invitation code?

Enter invitation code

Then add your Date of Birth and click 'Register'

Now you are logged in

Select your name

Select 'My enquiries' from the drop down menu

Click on 'Volunteer in new area'

Complete your details

  1. Enter your postcode
  2. Select section you're interested in
  3. Search
  4. Choose the unit you want to volunteer with.

Submit your enquiry

  1. Unit name appears in selection box
  2. Submit

You should receive an enquiry number - make a note of this.
