Are you passionate about growing guiding in Hertfordshire and finding new volunteers to join us and help offer opportunities to our young members?

Do you have what it takes to inspire new members and help retain existing volunteers within the county?

You will be a strong communicator with good IT skills, be well organised and approachable. If this sounds like you, you could be who we are looking for.

You will be responsible for promoting Girlguiding, including coordinating promotional stands at events such as the County Show, held over the May bank holiday weekend each year. In addition, you will support others organising their own recruitment events by offering advice and county branded promotional items for hire.

Further details of what the role entails can be found below.

Please apply via <vacancies@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk>.
