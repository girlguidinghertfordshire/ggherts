Would you like to play a vital role in helping us celebrate and recognise the achievements of our members and volunteers?

If you have good administrative skills and enjoy organising events, this role could be perfect for you.

As awards secretary lead volunteer you will provide administrative support to our county awards lead volunteer, take an active role in helping select which nominees receive awards and assist with organising the county's annual awards celebration event.

You can find out more about what the role entails below and apply via <vacancies@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk>
