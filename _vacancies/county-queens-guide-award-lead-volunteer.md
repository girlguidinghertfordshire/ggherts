Would you like to help young members aged 16-30 develop their skills as they work towards the highest award that can be achieved in guiding?

If you are a current adult member who has completed their Queen's Guide award or has knowledge of the requirements and expectations, this role could be for you.

As county Queen's Guide award lead volunteer you will oversee the award process and guide mentors supporting members complete the award.

You can find out more about what the role entails below and apply via <vacancies@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk>
