We are looking for an organised, enthusiastic, personable volunteer to be our next county badge secretary to act as point of contact for leaders, county teams and shops within Hertfordshire county wanting to order badges including, but not limited to, county badges, programme badges, section awards and long service awards.

As well as manning a county badge stall at events where asked, you will also oversee the shops within the county, relaying information from HQ and making sure all relevant paperwork is kept up to date.

You can find out more about what the role entails below and apply via <vacancies@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk>
