As well as promoting opportunities and planning county-wide events for Rangers the county Ranger lead volunteer works with Ranger unit leaders to ensure the Girlguiding programme is delivered effectively.

If you are passionate about retaining young members as they reach the end of their Guides experience and are either a current or former Ranger leader, have knowledge of the Ranger programme and the Ranger lead away qualification this is the perfect role for you.

You can find out more about what the role entails below and apply via <vacancies@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk>
