We are looking for someone who can motivate, inspire and build positive working relationship with others.

The county leader in training / leadership development programme coordinator plays an important part in ensuring the future of guiding in Hertfordshire. Providing support and encouragement to leaders in training (LiTs), aiding them on their learning journey to become unit leaders.

You will be responsible for matching leaders in training with their mentors, monitoring their progress and verifying leadership development programme (LDP) completions.

As use of technology forms a big part of this role you will need good IT skills and be confident in accessing Girlguiding platforms.

Further details of what the role entails can be found below.

Please apply via <vacancies@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk>.
