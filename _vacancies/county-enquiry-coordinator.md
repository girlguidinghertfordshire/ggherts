Acting as the first point of contact in Hertfordshire for new volunteer enquiries received via the online recruitment tool, you will help match potential volunteers, who have not been able to identify a volunteering vacancy, with the right opportunity to suit both them and the county's needs.

To apply for this role you must be aged 18 or over and have good communication, problem solving and IT skills, as you welcome new members to the county.

Further details of what the role entails can be found below.

Please apply via <vacancies@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk>
