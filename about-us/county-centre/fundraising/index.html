Easyfundraising

Sign up for Easyfundraising and select Hertfordshire Guide Centre as your charity.

Use this link to access us: http://efraising.org/DN1n03cgt6

Did you know you can support us and raise money every time you shop online? And the best part? It doesn’t cost a penny! Introducing Easyfundraising, the free fundraising platform that let’s us earn donations when we all shop online. With 8,100+ brands to choose from, including Tesco, Sainsbury’s, John Lewis & Partners, Sports Direct, ASOS, M&S, Boots, Just Eat, and Booking.com, there’s something for everyone.

Here’s how it works

  1. Download the Easyfundraising app,
  2. Sign up,
  3. Simply start your shopping journey and the retailer you shop with send us a free donation based on your spending and with no additional cost to you.

Registering yourself takes just 2 minutes and all you need is your email address.

Ways to donate

Our bank account

Hertfordshire Guide Centre also has it's own fundraising bank account which you can pay your donations into directly. Whether you wish to donate as an individual, or your unit/district/team have been fundraising and you are now ready to donate your profits, please email admin@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk for the bank account details.

Gift Aid

Gift Aid is a scheme that allows charities to claim an extra 25p for every £1 you donate. As a registered charity Girlguiding Hertfordshire would be thankful if you chose us, if you don't already Gift Aid to anyone else.

You need to make a declaration to your chosen charity and pay enough tax to qualify. Please email your completed forms to admin@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk.
