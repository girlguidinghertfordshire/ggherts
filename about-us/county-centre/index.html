The Hertfordshire Guide Centre is located in the village of Cottered in North Herts. Owned by Girlguiding Hertfordshire, it is suitable for meetings, trainings, sleepovers and camps.

Hertfordshire Guide Centre campsite/field is now available for booking, and more information can be found on the website’s Latest News page. The indoor accommodation currently remains closed. Please contact the booking secretary for more details and availability.

The remote County Office’s current term time only working hours are:

Mondays and Thursdays: 9.30am – 5.00pm

Tuesdays and Wednesdays: 9.30am – 2.00pm

Fridays: 9.30am – 1.30pm

If a visit to the site is required please contact the county office to arrange a date and time.

Address: County Office c/o Hertfordshire Guide Centre, Cottered, Buntingford, Hertfordshire, SG9 9QP
Email: countysec@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk
Website: www.hertsguidecentre.co.uk
