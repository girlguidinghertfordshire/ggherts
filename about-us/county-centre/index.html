The Hertfordshire Guide Centre is located in the village of Cottered in North Herts. Owned by Girlguiding Hertfordshire, it is suitable for meetings, trainings, sleepovers and camps.

Hertfordshire Guide Centre campsite/field is now available for booking, and more information can be found on the latest news page. The indoor accommodation currently remains closed. Please contact the booking secretary for more details and availability.

If a visit to the site is required please contact the HGC Bookings Secretary to arrange a date and time.

Address: Hertfordshire Guide Centre, Cottered, Buntingford, Hertfordshire, SG9 9QP
Email: HGCBookings@girlguidinghertfordshire.org.uk
Website: www.hertsguidecentre.co.uk
