Our fully accessible and inclusive holiday home is perfect for unit and district holidays and other indoor residentials, as well as daytime and evening events.

## Facilities

### Sleeping accommodation

Girlguiding Hertfordshire is excited to be able to offer 5 bedrooms (13 bunk beds and 1 single bed with hoist). For larger groups, there are extra mattresses located under bunk beds which can be used in the bedrooms or in the main hall.

| Bedroom | Sleeps | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Lea | 10 | Adjoining sink |
| Colne | 6 | Spacious room, fire door |
| Mimram | 4 | Next to shower room (might be preferable as a leader room) |
| Ver | 4 | Small room (original leader room) |
| Beane | 3 | Hoist and en-suite accessible shower room |

### Washrooms

- New: fully accessible shower room with toilet, sink, adult changing table (with shower function) and hoist.
- New: shower room with toilet and sink.
- 2 separate rooms containing toilets and sinks (total 5 toilets).
- 2 additional individual toilets and sinks.

### Kitchen

- 2 cookers.
- 2 fridges.
- Drawer freezer.
- Microwave.
- Hot cupboard (users should be aware this remains hot for some time after use).
- Utensils and crockery.
- Serving hatch onto the main hall.
- Low level worktop for children to prepare food.

### Main hall

- Large space for games and activities (10.20m x 9.38m).
- Positioned centrally in the building for easy access to bedrooms, washrooms and the kitchen.
- Beautiful parque floor.
- Noticeboards.
- Hatch through to kitchen.

### A spacious entrance hall

- Wide entrance with ramp.
- Equipped with coat hooks.

### Media

- Free WiFi.

## Additional information

- Hot water and heating included.
- Access to the trim trail and campfire circle is shared with campsite guests.
- Shop – with badges and other Girlguiding Hertfordshire merchandise.
