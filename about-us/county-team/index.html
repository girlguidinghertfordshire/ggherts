Division commissioners

Girlguiding Hertfordshire is divided into 10 divisions, each being overseen by either 1, or joint, division commissioner(s) who leads a team of dedicated and skilled volunteers to ensure quality guiding is delivered locally.

With the exception of the flexible division, each division contains many districts which support local units and events.

Division commissioner vacancies can be found on the vacancies webpage

County vice presidents

County vice presidents act as ambassadors for Girlguiding in Hertfordshire county.

Trefoil Guild

Trefoil Guild is a branch of the Guide Association for everyone aged 18+ which is administered independently.

It is a registered charity in its own right, which aims to carry the spirit of guiding into the community.
